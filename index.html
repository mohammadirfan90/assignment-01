Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Driving progress through creative solutions and advanced technologies to foster growth and competitiveness.

Sustainability

Ensuring balanced development while preserving resources for future generations and protecting the environment.

Infrastructure

Building robust systems and facilities to support economic growth and societal well-being.

Empowerment

Promoting inclusivity, education, and opportunities to uplift individuals and communities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

I envision a "New Bangladesh" harnessed with innovation, inclusivity, and sustainability. My dream is to see a nation free from poverty and inequality, where individuals, especially women, are empowered through education and entrepreneurship. We can change challenges into opportunities: a society in which wealth will be equitably distributed, not concentrated in a few hands. A new Bangladesh must embrace the power of youth, the strength of technology, and the promise of social business. Let us strive together to create a future of dignity, equality, and harmony with nature for all.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
